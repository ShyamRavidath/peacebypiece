# Page Topology — Peace by Piece (home)

Target: https://peacebypieceor.wixsite.com/peace-by-piece
Single-page static marketing site. No scroll-driven or click-driven state. Interaction model: **static + hover only**.

## Sections (top → bottom)
1. **Header** (sticky banner) — dark-green `#3E6E64` bar. Left: small logo glyph + "Peace by Piece" wordmark (Fraunces). Center: nav (Home, About, Meet The Team, Get Involved, Events, Contact). Right: Log In (user icon), Donate button (brown `#463831`).
2. **Hero** — full-width classroom photo bg with brown overlay. Centered serif h1 "Peace by Piece" (106px Fraunces, `#463831`), h6 subtitle "Piecing Together Systemic Education Gap". Left vertical social bar (TikTok, Instagram).
3. **Quick-link cards** — 3-col row. About Us / "Our Vision and Mission" (`#3E6E64`), Get Involved / "Support and Volunteer" (lighter `#4B8D77`, highlighted), Events / "What We've Been Up To" (`#3E6E64`). Cream `#EAF0E9` text.
4. **Mission** — 2-col split. Left dark brown `#463831` panel with "Piecing Everything Together" (56px Fraunces, cream). Right light panel with "Our Mission" h3 + paragraph.
5. **Tagline band** — green `#4B8D77` full-width band, centered italic serif: "We fight for our cause at three intersecting levels: systemic, educational, and direct impact." (34px Fraunces).
6. **CTA** — green `#4B8D77` band. Left: "Join Our Cause" (60px Fraunces), "Support Educational Equity", Donate Now button (brown). Right: hands photo.
7. **Footer** — light mint `#EAF0E9` contact band (email, socials) + green `#4B8D77` copyright bar (© Peace by Piece, Terms & Conditions | Privacy Policy | Accessibility Statement).

## Layout
- Max content width ~1200px, centered. Full-bleed colored bands.
- Responsive: stack columns on mobile (<768px). Original Wix served near-identical desktop layout at 390px; clone will stack sensibly.

## Z-index
- Header sticky top. Hero social bar floats over photo. No modals (Wix chat widget excluded).
