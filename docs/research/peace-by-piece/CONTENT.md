## About — "Our Impact" (founded June 2024, ~1 year)
- 8 high school Executive Board members + 50+ high school volunteers
- 8+ community events (farmers markets, bake sales) — thousands reached via brochures, flyers, conversation
- 100+ bracelets for children
- 100+ cards for underprivileged youth
- 50+ mental health care packages donated to OHSU patients
- Partnered with Family Promise — raised $600–700 in school supplies
- 5+ hours organizing the Family Promise shelter
- Fundraised almost $2000 from events
- Supported by BSD Board Member Van Truong (now advisor/consultant)
- In progress: AI Mental Health Tool impact research paper (w/ university professor), children's book on mental well-being, elementary school projects/presentations in BSD
- Problem statement: "Educational curricula are often created at the federal and state levels, resulting in them being both surface-level and not culturally sensitive to many students. In addition, educational support for low-income and low-risk students is often inaccessible because of time, fiscal, and stigmatizing barriers."
